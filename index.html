Current Research: Automatic Colourisation

Developing methods to enhance the state-of-the-art in both automatic image colourisation and automatic video colourisation. Some of the key topics being explored are cantered around temporal consistency in video colourisation and personalisation of colourisation.

Fourth-Year Project: Locale-specific deep learning colourisation applied to NLI/NFC/NUI Galway Archives resources

The focus of this project was to re-train a popular automatic black and white photograph colourisation model to be more accurate on Irish photographs. This was to be achieved by balancing generic training data sets, e.g. OpenImages, with Irish ones, e.g. National Library of Ireland.
